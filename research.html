LivWell App

I worked as an app developer for the LivWell App from May 2018 to August 2018. More specifically, my role was coding a spatial analysis tab using R Shiny App packages to allow various researchers and organizations to visually analyze the impact of their forest development efforts. This project allowed me to work both independently and collaborate and communicate as part of an international team.
