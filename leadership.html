Girls Who Code

As a chair, I aid in running the University of Michigan branch of Girls Who Code, an organization aimed at supporting and increasing the number of women in tech fields through hosting coding workshops, social events, and community outreach programs.


Global Scholars Program

As a collaborative group facilitator for the Global Scholars Program, I lead a group of underclassmen to help them complete an internship with an international organization, facilitating their development of professional skills as well as a better understanding of global social justice.


University Unions League Board of Governors

As the chair of the board, I lead a group of students, staff, and alumni to discuss and reach consensus about decisions regarding a historical university union, including renovations, policy change, and scholarship distribution.
